# AI Voice Receptionist & Lead Qualification System

A production-ready AI voice agent that autonomously handles inbound phone calls for field-service and skilled trades businesses.

### What It Does
- Answers calls 24/7 with natural, professional voice conversation
- Qualifies every caller through intelligent, structured dialogue
- Books appointments and service estimates directly into the company calendar
- Captures complete lead information and routes it to the business owner
- Scores leads in real time and triggers the appropriate follow-up workflow
- Operates completely hands-free so no calls are ever missed

### Key Benefits
- Eliminates lost revenue from unanswered or after-hours calls
- Delivers only pre-qualified, warm leads to the service team
- Reduces the need for full-time receptionist staff
- Provides consistent, professional customer experience at every hour of the day

### Core Capabilities
- Real-time natural voice conversation with objection handling
- Intelligent lead scoring and multi-path routing logic
- Direct calendar integration for instant booking
- Automated SMS and email notifications with full call summaries
- Built-in compliance handling for consent and call recording

Designed specifically for high-intent service trades where every inbound call represents potential revenue and speed-to-response is critical.

**Status:** Production-ready MVP
